Contact Calc Nest

Questions, feedback, or ideas for a new calculator or guide?

Calc Nest is a growing collection of free online calculators and practical guides. If you want to suggest a new tool, report an issue, point out something unclear, or share general feedback about the site, you can get in touch by email.

Feedback is especially useful when it relates to calculator clarity, guide usefulness, layout issues, broken links, mobile usability, or ideas for topics that would make Calc Nest more helpful over time.

Contact email

The simplest way to reach Calc Nest is by email.

Email churdomains@gmail.com

Calc Nest welcomes questions, suggestions, bug reports, and feedback about calculators, guides, categories, and overall site experience.

What you can contact Calc Nest about

Some of the most useful messages are the practical ones.

Calculator suggestions

If there is a calculator you would like to see added to the site, you can suggest it by email. Topic ideas across finance, health, dates, percentages, taxes, or everyday calculations are all relevant.

Guide or content suggestions

If there is a concept that feels confusing or deserves its own guide—such as borrowing, inflation, repayment, affordability, or percentage interpretation—you can suggest that as well.

Site issues or feedback

Broken links, confusing explanations, mobile layout problems, incorrect assumptions, or general usability feedback are all useful kinds of messages.

Useful things to include in your message

A little detail makes feedback much easier to act on.

For calculator or guide feedback

  • the page or calculator name
  • what looked unclear or incorrect
  • what result or behavior you expected
  • whether the issue happened on mobile or desktop

For new ideas

  • the calculator or guide topic you want
  • what kind of problem it would help solve
  • who you think would use it
  • any example use case that would make it especially useful

Clear, practical suggestions are especially helpful because Calc Nest is designed around real user use cases rather than filler pages.

What Contact is for—and what it is not for

A short note on scope.

Contact is intended for site-related questions, suggestions, corrections, and feedback. It is not a channel for personalized medical, legal, financial, tax, or investment advice.

Calc Nest provides general educational tools and practical guides, but important personal decisions should still be evaluated in the context of your own situation and, when needed, with qualified professionals.
