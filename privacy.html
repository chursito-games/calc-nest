Privacy Policy

How Calc Nest handles calculator inputs, cookies, and third-party services

This Privacy Policy explains, in general terms, how information may be handled when you visit Calc Nest and use its calculators or guides.

Calc Nest is designed to be simple to use. The site does not require users to create an account in order to access calculators or read guide pages, and the goal is to keep usage as lightweight as possible.

Last updated: April 2026

Privacy summary

No account required Calc Nest does not require account creation to use the site.
Calculator inputs Calculator values are intended to be processed directly in your browser for normal use.
Advertising and cookies Third-party services such as Google may use cookies and related technologies for advertising and measurement.
Questions You can contact Calc Nest through the Contact page.

Information users provide directly

Calc Nest is designed so most visitors can use the site without submitting personal information directly.

Calc Nest does not require users to create an account or sign in to use calculators or read guide pages.

If you choose to contact Calc Nest, any information you include in your message—such as your email address, name, or the contents of your message—may be received and used to respond to your inquiry, review feedback, or improve the site.

Calculator inputs

A core goal of Calc Nest is to keep calculator usage lightweight and browser-based.

Under normal use, values entered into calculators are intended to be processed directly in your browser so results can be displayed on the page.

Calc Nest does not require users to create profiles or save calculator histories in order to use the site.

Users should still avoid entering highly sensitive personal information into general-purpose calculators unless they are comfortable doing so.

External links

Some pages on Calc Nest may include links to other websites.

If you follow a link to an external site, that site may have its own privacy practices, terms, cookies, and tracking technologies. Calc Nest is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of third-party websites.

Children’s privacy

A general note on audience and usage.

Calc Nest is intended as a general informational website and is not designed specifically for children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe a child has provided personal information through direct contact with the site, you may use the Contact page to reach out.

Frequently asked questions

Common privacy-related questions about Calc Nest.

Do I need an account to use Calc Nest?

No. Calc Nest does not require users to create an account in order to use the calculators or read the guides.

Are calculator values stored in an account?

Calc Nest does not require account-based storage for normal calculator use. Calculator values are intended to be processed directly in the browser for general use.

Does Calc Nest use cookies?

The site and third-party services used on the site may use cookies or similar technologies for functionality, advertising, measurement, and related purposes.

Can third parties use cookies on this site?

Yes. Where third-party advertising or related services are used, those third parties may place or read cookies or use similar technologies in connection with ad serving and measurement. :contentReference[oaicite:7]{index=7}

Can I turn off personalized ads?

Google says users can manage ad personalization through Ad Settings / My Ad Center, although ads may still appear even when personalized ads are turned off. :contentReference[oaicite:8]{index=8}

How can I contact Calc Nest about privacy questions?

You can use the Contact page to get in touch regarding site-related questions or feedback.
