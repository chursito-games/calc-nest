What is a tip calculator?

A tip calculator helps estimate how much to leave as a gratuity based on the bill amount and your chosen percentage. It can also split the final amount fairly across multiple people.

This is useful when dining out, sharing restaurant bills, ordering food delivery, or handling service-related tipping situations.

How tip calculations work

To calculate a tip, multiply the bill amount by the tip percentage and divide by 100. Add that value to the original bill to get the total amount.

If the bill is split, divide the tip and total amount by the number of people.

Common tip percentages

Tipping customs vary by country and service type, but common restaurant tip percentages often include:

  • 10% for basic service
  • 15% for standard service
  • 18% to 20% for good service
  • 25% or more for exceptional service

Frequently asked questions

How do I calculate a tip manually?

Multiply the bill amount by the tip percentage and divide by 100. Then add that tip to the original bill.

What does “round up total per person” do?

It rounds the total each person pays up to the next whole number, which can make payment simpler when splitting a bill.

Can I use this calculator for any currency?

Yes. The calculator uses plain numbers, so it can work with dollars, euros, pounds, pesos, or other currencies.

Does this calculator store my data?

No. The values you enter are processed directly in your browser and are not stored on a server.
