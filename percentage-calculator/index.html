How percentage calculations work

A percentage represents a proportion out of 100. For example, 20% means 20 out of every 100 units.

To calculate what percentage of a number is, multiply the number by the percentage and divide by 100.

Example:

20% of 150 = 150 × 0.20 = 30

Percentage increases and decreases compare the difference between two values relative to the original value.
