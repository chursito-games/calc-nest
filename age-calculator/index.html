How the age calculation works

This age calculator compares your birth date with the current date and determines the difference in years, months, and days. It also calculates the total amount of time that has passed in days, hours, minutes, and seconds.

For example, if someone was born on January 1, 2000 and today is January 1, 2024, their age would be exactly 24 years.
